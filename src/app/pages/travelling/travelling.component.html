Options

Flying to Václav Havel Airport Prague.

Once at the Airport you need to get the to Center.
Getting to the Center:
1. Buy a ticket for the AE airport bus asking to get off at Hlavní nádraží.
2. At Hlavní nádraží, board your Train to Ostrava(hl.vn) (RJ) / or even after at Bohumnia(Leo express or Cseke Drahy).
(optional): rent a car if you prefer to drive there.
If you plan is Ostrava
Get a bus from Centre to Venue.
If you plan is Bohumnia
Get train to the venue and walk.
